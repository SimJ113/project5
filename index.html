Top 3 Nintendo Switch Games

Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild was a revolutionary game that released March 3, 2017. With its introduction to the open-world of Hyrule, Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild was at the top of gaming at the time.

Its physics-based interactions through special abilities and the potential to craft many items and explore across unique terrains using gliders and climbing made the experience ever more unique. As a number 1 game, Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild fits that like a glove.

Mario Kart 8 Deluxe

Mario Kart 8 Deluxe, released on April 28, 2017, is an enhanced version of the Wii U game Mario Kart 8. It includes all the previous downloadable content, additional characters, and a revamped battle mode.

The game is praised for its beautiful graphics, tight controls, and the sheer fun of racing with friends and family. With new characters and courses, Mario Kart 8 Deluxe offers endless hours of entertainment.

Source: YouTube Video

Super Mario Party

Super Mario Party, released on October 5, 2018, brings the classic board game style back to the franchise with a variety of new features and mini-games.

The game is designed for both local and online multiplayer, making it a great choice for parties and gatherings. With innovative use of the Joy-Con controllers, Super Mario Party offers a fresh take on the beloved series.
